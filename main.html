Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, the ancient capital of Japan, is a city where tradition and modernity coexist harmoniously. Known for its classical Buddhist temples, stunning gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses, Kyoto offers a unique glimpse into Japan's rich cultural heritage. The city is also famous for its festivals, tea ceremonies, and geisha culture, making it a must-visit destination for those who wish to experience the essence of Japan.

  1. Fushimi Inari Taisha
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  4. Kiyomizu-dera Temple
  5. Nijo Castle
  • Kaiseki
  • Yudofu (Boiled Tofu)
  • Matcha (Green Tea) Sweets
  • Nishin Soba
  • Kyo-Wagash
